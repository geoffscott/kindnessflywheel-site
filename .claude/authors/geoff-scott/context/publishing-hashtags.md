# Publishing Hashtags — The Kindness Flywheel

Five lenses for storytelling. Every post is tagged with one or more of these.

---

## The Five Lenses

### #Strategy
Convergence, trust, competitive advantage, the business case for kindness. The strategic argument for why this matters now.

### #People
Education, professional development, culture, and behavior.

### #Technology
Product design, implementation, security and compliance, agent development. The craft of building with care.

### #Practice
Real organizational stories. What happened when we tried this. Outcomes, surprises, failures, lessons from OneEleven, Saranam, SIMNY, CFOKit, Table, and contributors' own organizations.

### #Meta
How this publication works. Content philosophy, AI copyright, GEO strategy, editorial process, contributor governance. The self-aware layer.

---

## Publishing Strategy

**All posts should:**
- Use 1-3 lenses based on the post's focus
- Be grounded in lived experience, not theory
- Tell the real story — what happened, what you learned, what surprised you

**Example combinations:**
- Inaugural thesis post: #Strategy
- OpenClaw deployment in SOC 2 environment: #Technology
- OneEleven coaching culture story: #Practice
- Teaching kids empathy in the AI era: #People
- Why we chose CC-BY 4.0 and what it means for AI citation: #Meta
- How trust drove retention at OneEleven: #Strategy + #Practice
- Building the Growth Science site with agents: #Technology + #Practice
