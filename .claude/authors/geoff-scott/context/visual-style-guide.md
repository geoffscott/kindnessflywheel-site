## Implementation for Growth Science

### Homepage Hero
- Use similar professional, aspirational imagery
- Apply primary blue (`#0082f3`) as accent color in CTAs
- Yellow (`#cadb3a`) for secondary emphasis

### Navigation & Buttons
- Background: Dark colors (`#0e0d0a`, `#071e22`)
- Text: Light (`#f6f6f6`, `#fafafa`)
- Hover state: Primary blue (`#0082f3`)
- CTA buttons: Yellow (`#cadb3a`) background, dark text

### Content Sections
- Alternating light (`#fafafa`) and white (`#ffffff`) backgrounds
- Dark text (`#0e0d0a` or `#2d2d2d`) for readability
- Accent headers in primary blue (`#0082f3`)

### Links
- Color: Primary blue (`#0082f3`)
- Hover: Darken or use accent orange (`#e86035`)

## Accessibility

- Contrast ratios meet WCAG AA standards
- Text sizes follow Roboto defaults (14px-18px body, 24px+ headings)
- Color is not the only indicator (underlines for links, etc.)
