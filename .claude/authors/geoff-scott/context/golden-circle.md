# Growth Science — Golden Circle

Based on Simon Sinek's Start with Why framework. The concentric circles that define Growth Science's positioning.

---

## Why

In an age where technical capability is commoditized, leaders need to build organizations grounded in trust, clarity, and genuine human connection. This is how sustainable competitive advantage actually works.

---

## How

Through dialogue that helps you see yourself and your situation more clearly—so you can make choices that align with what actually matters to you. My work helps you become conscious of what's driving your decisions so you can lead and live with intention rather than by default.

---

## What

Executive coaching and advisory services.

---

## How This Guides Everything

The **Why** is the thesis underlying The Kindness Flywheel and all strategic positioning.

The **How** is what differentiates Growth Science from other executive coaches. It's not about fixing you or teaching you techniques. It's about clarity through dialogue, so you can choose consciously.

The **What** is the practical offering—but it flows from Why and How, not the other way around.

Every page on the site, every conversation, every piece of content should reflect this circle. The reader should feel: "Yes, I want more clarity. Yes, I want to choose consciously. Yes, I want to lead with intention."
